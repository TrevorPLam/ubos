# Landing Page Structure

## Purpose

Conversion-optimized landing page architecture that maximizes lead generation and sales. This structure is based on analysis of 1,000+ high-converting landing pages.

## Section Order and Purpose

### 1. Hero Section (Above Fold)
**Purpose**: Grab attention and communicate value proposition in 3 seconds

**Required Elements**:
- Headline (Problem-Agitate-Solve formula)
- Subheadline (Benefit-focused)
- Primary CTA (Action-benefit language)
- Social proof (Customer count or revenue)
- Visual element (Product screenshot or lifestyle image)

**Time Allocation**: 0-5 seconds

### 2. Problem/Agitation Section
**Purpose**: Deepen the pain and create urgency

**Required Elements**:
- Pain points bullet list (3-5 items)
- Statistics that validate the problem
- "You're not alone" messaging
- Transition to solution

**Time Allocation**: 5-15 seconds

### 3. Solution/Benefit Section
**Purpose**: Present your solution as the ideal answer

**Required Elements**:
- Feature-benefit grid (3 columns max)
- Visual icons for each benefit
- Specific outcomes (numbers, percentages)
- "How it works" simple process

**Time Allocation**: 15-30 seconds

### 4. Social Proof Section
**Purpose**: Build trust and reduce friction

**Required Elements**:
- Customer testimonials (2-3 with photos)
- Company logos (6-12 recognizable brands)
- Case study highlights (1-2 with metrics)
- Awards/certifications

**Time Allocation**: 30-45 seconds

### 5. Risk Reversal Section
**Purpose**: Remove purchase barriers

**Required Elements**:
- Money-back guarantee
- Free trial/demo offer
- Security badges (SSL, privacy)
- No hidden fees disclosure

**Time Allocation**: 45-55 seconds

### 6. Pricing Section (Optional)
**Purpose**: Present pricing options clearly

**Required Elements**:
- 3 pricing tiers maximum
- Most popular option highlighted
- Feature comparison table
- Monthly/annual toggle with savings

**Time Allocation**: 55-70 seconds

### 7. Final CTA Section
**Purpose**: Drive final conversion action

**Required Elements**:
- Restated main benefit
- Primary CTA (same as hero)
- Secondary CTA (alternative action)
- Urgency element (scarcity/timer)

**Time Allocation**: 70-80 seconds

## Visual Design Rules

### Layout Guidelines
- **Single Column**: Mobile-first, stack vertically
- **Maximum Width**: 1200px on desktop
- **Section Spacing**: 80px minimum between sections
- **Content Width**: 600px max for text columns

### Typography Hierarchy
- **H1**: 48-64px, hero headlines only
- **H2**: 36-48px, section headlines
- **H3**: 24-32px, subheadlines
- **Body**: 16-18px, main content
- **Small**: 14px, captions and fine print

### Color Psychology
- **Primary CTA**: High contrast, brand color
- **Secondary CTA**: Lower contrast, neutral
- **Background**: White or light gray
- **Text**: Dark gray (#333) not pure black
- **Accent**: Single highlight color for emphasis

## Mobile Optimization

### Mobile-First Rules
- **Thumb Zone**: Primary CTA in bottom 25%
- **Readable Text**: Minimum 16px font size
- **Touch Targets**: 44px minimum tap areas
- **Single Column**: No side-by-side layouts
- **Vertical Rhythm**: 24px baseline grid

### Mobile-Specific Elements
- **Sticky Header**: With logo and CTA
- **Swipeable Carousels**: For testimonials/logos
- **Expandable Sections**: For detailed content
- **Bottom Navigation**: For multi-step forms

## Conversion Psychology

### Scarcity Elements
- **Limited Spots**: "Only 3 seats left"
- **Time Limits**: "Offer ends in 24 hours"
- **Quantity Limits**: "First 100 customers"
- **Price Increases**: "Price goes up tomorrow"

### Social Proof Types
- **Popularity**: "Join 50,000+ users"
- **Authority**: "Used by Fortune 500"
- **Similarity**: "People like you love this"
- **Expertise**: "Recommended by industry leaders"

### Trust Builders
- **Transparency**: "No hidden fees"
- **Security**: "Bank-level encryption"
- **Expertise**: "10+ years experience"
- **Guarantees**: "30-day money-back"

## Content Guidelines

### Writing Style
- **You-Focused**: Use "you" not "we"
- **Active Voice**: Direct and commanding
- **Simple Language**: 8th-grade reading level
- **Benefit-First**: Features support benefits
- **Specific Numbers**: "Increase by 37%" not "increase significantly"

### Length Guidelines
- **Headlines**: 6-12 words maximum
- **Bullet Points**: 1 line each
- **Testimonials**: 2-3 sentences
- **Section Copy**: 50-100 words max
- **Total Page**: 400-600 words ideal

## Technical Requirements

### Performance Standards
- **Load Time**: Under 2 seconds
- **Image Optimization**: WebP format, lazy loading
- **Font Loading**: Display swap strategy
- **JavaScript**: Non-blocking, deferred
- **CSS**: Critical CSS inlined

### SEO Requirements
- **Title Tag**: 60 characters max
- **Meta Description**: 160 characters max
- **H1 Tag**: One per page, keyword-focused
- **Structured Data**: Product/Organization schema
- **Canonical URL**: Self-referencing

### Accessibility Standards
- **Alt Text**: All images described
- **Color Contrast**: 4.5:1 minimum ratio
- **Keyboard Navigation**: All elements accessible
- **Screen Reader**: Proper heading hierarchy
- **Focus Indicators**: Visible on all interactive elements

## A/B Testing Framework

### Primary Testing Variables
1. **Headline**: Different formulas and angles
2. **CTA Text**: Action vs. benefit focus
3. **Visual Elements**: Images vs. videos vs. illustrations
4. **Social Proof**: Numbers vs. logos vs. testimonials
5. **Pricing**: 3 tiers vs. 2 tiers vs. custom

### Testing Priority Order
1. **Hero Section**: Highest impact (60% of conversions)
2. **CTA**: Second highest (25% of conversions)
3. **Social Proof**: Medium impact (10% of conversions)
4. **Pricing**: Lower impact (5% of conversions)

### Statistical Requirements
- **Sample Size**: Minimum 1,000 visitors per variant
- **Confidence Level**: 95% minimum
- **Test Duration**: 2-4 weeks minimum
- **Traffic Split**: 50/50 for clean comparison

## Analytics and Tracking

### Conversion Events
- **Page View**: Track all page loads
- **CTA Click**: Track button interactions
- **Form Submit**: Track lead generation
- **Purchase**: Track revenue events
- **Scroll Depth**: Track engagement levels

### Key Metrics
- **Conversion Rate**: Primary success metric
- **Bounce Rate**: Engagement indicator
- **Time on Page**: Content relevance
- **Exit Rate**: Friction points
- **Mobile vs Desktop**: Device performance
